Troubleshooting

App crashes or freezes

  • • Make sure you have the latest version of the app
  • • Restart your device
  • • Free up storage space on your device
  • • Update your iOS to the latest version

Questions not loading

  • • Check your internet connection
  • • Force close and reopen the app
  • • Clear the app cache in Settings
  • • Reinstall the app if the issue persists

Statistics not updating

  • • Ensure you're completing tests properly
  • • Check that you're signed in with the same account
  • • Try syncing from Settings > Sync Data
  • • Contact support if data is missing
